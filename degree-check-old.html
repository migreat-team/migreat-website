About Degree Recognition

What is anabin?
anabin is the official German database for the recognition of foreign educational qualifications. It's maintained by the Central Office for Foreign Education (ZAB).

Why is recognition important?
Having your degree recognized in Germany is often required for regulated professions (like doctors, nurses, engineers) and can significantly improve your chances of getting a work visa.

What if my degree isn't recognized?
Even if your degree isn't automatically recognized, you may still have options such as the Opportunity Card, vocational training, or applying for individual assessment.
